What Does It Mean to Nest HTML Tags?


  • The easiest way to understand nesting is to think of HTML tags as boxes that hold your content.
  • Your content can include text, images, and related media. HTML tags are the boxes around the content.
  • Sometimes, you need to places boxes inside of other boxes. Those "inner" boxes are nested inside of others.
  • Nested tags are comes with display inline property
