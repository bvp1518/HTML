What is Inline element in HTML?

Inline elements are elements whose horizontal width is determined by the width of the content they contain.

For example, This HTML elements <b> , <abbr> , <i> , <span> and <em> are inline-level elements that are't place their content onto a new line.It is opposite of block level elemnts
